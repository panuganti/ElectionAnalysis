Lakhisarai District : Census 2011 data

Lakhisarai District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Lakhisarai, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Lakhisarai District of Bihar.

Lakhisarai District Population 2011

In 2011, Lakhisarai had population of 1,000,912 of which male and female were 526,345 and 474,567 respectively. In 2001 census, Lakhisarai had a population of 802,225 of which males were 417,672 and remaining 384,553 were females. Lakhisarai District population constituted 0.96 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Lakhisarai District was at 0.97 percent of Maharashtra population.

Lakhisarai District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 24.77 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Lakhisarai District recorded increase of 23.94 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Lakhisarai District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Lakhisarai district for 2011 is 815 people per sq. km. In 2001, Lakhisarai district density was at 653 people per sq. km. Lakhisarai district administers 1,228 square kilometers of areas.

Lakhisarai Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Lakhisarai in 2011 were 62.42 compared to 47.96 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 71.26 and 52.57 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 60.74 and 33.96 in Lakhisarai District. Total literate in Lakhisarai District were 507,270 of which male and female were 305,182 and 202,088 respectively. In 2001, Lakhisarai District had 303,536 in its district.

Lakhisarai Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Lakhisarai, it stood at 902 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 921. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 920 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 951 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Lakhisarai Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Lakhisarai. There were total 188,214 children under age of 0-6 against 169,320 of 2001 census. Of total 188,214 male and female were 98,050 and 90,164 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 920 compared to 951 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 18.80 percent of Lakhisarai District compared to 21.11 percent of 2001. There was net change of -2.31 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Lakhisarai District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Lakhisarai population for 2011 census, 14.29 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 143,011 people lives in urban areas of which males are 75,482 and females are 67,529. Sex Ratio in urban region of Lakhisarai district is 895 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Lakhisarai district was 884 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 24,347 of which males and females were 12,926 and 11,421. This child population figure of Lakhisarai district is 17.12 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Lakhisarai district as per census 2011 is 71.33 % of which males and females are 78.39 % and 63.47 % literates respectively. In actual number 84,648 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 49,037 and 35,611 respectively.

Lakhisarai District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 85.71 % population of Lakhisarai districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Lakhisarai district population living in rural areas is 857,901 of which males and females are 450,863 and 407,038 respectively. In rural areas of Lakhisarai district, sex ratio is 903 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Lakhisarai district is considered, figure is 925 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 163,867 in rural areas of which males were 85,124 and females were 78,743. The child population comprises 18.88 % of total rural population of Lakhisarai district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Lakhisarai district is 60.89 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 70.03 and 50.71 percent respectively. In total, 422,622 people were literate of which males and females were 256,145 and 166,477 respectively.
