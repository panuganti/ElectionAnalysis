Matiaria Population - Pashchim Champaran, Bihar

Overview

Matiaria is small village located in Lauriya Block of Pashchim Champaran district, Bihar with total 692 families residing. The Matiaria village has population of 3376 of which 1731 are males while 1645 are females as per Population Census 2011.

In Matiaria village population of children with age 0-6 is 635 which makes up 18.81 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Matiaria village is 950 which is higher than Bihar state average of 918. Child Sex Ratio for the Matiaria as per census is 978, higher than Bihar average of 935.

Matiaria village has lower literacy rate compared to Bihar. In 2011, literacy rate of Matiaria village was 52.39 % compared to 61.80 % of Bihar. In Matiaria Male literacy stands at 63.05 % while female literacy rate was 41.10 %.

As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Matiaria village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.

Caste Factor

Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 14.96 % while Schedule Tribe (ST) were 6.55 % of total population in Matiaria village.

Work Profile

In Matiaria village out of total population, 1471 were engaged in work activities. 53.30 % of workers describe their work as Main Work (Employment or Earning more than 6 Months) while 46.70 % were involved in Marginal activity providing livelihood for less than 6 months. Of 1471 workers engaged in Main Work, 101 were cultivators (owner or co-owner) while 607 were Agricultural labourer.
