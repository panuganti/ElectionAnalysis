Bal Kunda Population - Khagaria, Bihar

Overview

Bal Kunda is small village located in Chautham Block of Khagaria district, Bihar with total 845 families residing. The Bal Kunda village has population of 4073 of which 2172 are males while 1901 are females as per Population Census 2011.

In Bal Kunda village population of children with age 0-6 is 895 which makes up 21.97 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Bal Kunda village is 875 which is lower than Bihar state average of 918. Child Sex Ratio for the Bal Kunda as per census is 984, higher than Bihar average of 935.

Bal Kunda village has lower literacy rate compared to Bihar. In 2011, literacy rate of Bal Kunda village was 46.60 % compared to 61.80 % of Bihar. In Bal Kunda Male literacy stands at 55.37 % while female literacy rate was 36.24 %.

As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Bal Kunda village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.

Caste Factor

Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 19.59 % of total population in Bal Kunda village. The village Bal Kunda currently doesn’t have any Schedule Tribe (ST) population.

Work Profile

In Bal Kunda village out of total population, 1981 were engaged in work activities. 41.29 % of workers describe their work as Main Work (Employment or Earning more than 6 Months) while 58.71 % were involved in Marginal activity providing livelihood for less than 6 months. Of 1981 workers engaged in Main Work, 178 were cultivators (owner or co-owner) while 359 were Agricultural labourer.
