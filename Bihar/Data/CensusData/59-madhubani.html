Madhubani District : Census 2011 data

Madhubani District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Madhubani, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Madhubani District of Bihar.

Madhubani District Population 2011

In 2011, Madhubani had population of 4,487,379 of which male and female were 2,329,313 and 2,158,066 respectively. In 2001 census, Madhubani had a population of 3,575,281 of which males were 1,840,997 and remaining 1,734,284 were females. Madhubani District population constituted 4.31 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Madhubani District was at 4.31 percent of Maharashtra population.

Madhubani District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 25.51 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Madhubani District recorded increase of 26.08 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Madhubani District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Madhubani district for 2011 is 1,282 people per sq. km. In 2001, Madhubani district density was at 1,021 people per sq. km. Madhubani district administers 3,501 square kilometers of areas.

Madhubani Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Madhubani in 2011 were 58.62 compared to 41.97 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 70.14 and 46.16 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 56.79 and 26.25 in Madhubani District. Total literate in Madhubani District were 2,155,338 of which male and female were 1,340,085 and 815,253 respectively. In 2001, Madhubani District had 1,195,776 in its district.

Madhubani Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Madhubani, it stood at 926 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 942. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 936 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 939 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Madhubani Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Madhubani. There were total 810,479 children under age of 0-6 against 725,999 of 2001 census. Of total 810,479 male and female were 418,616 and 391,863 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 936 compared to 939 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 18.06 percent of Madhubani District compared to 20.31 percent of 2001. There was net change of -2.25 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Madhubani District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Madhubani population for 2011 census, 3.60 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 161,495 people lives in urban areas of which males are 85,026 and females are 76,469. Sex Ratio in urban region of Madhubani district is 899 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Madhubani district was 925 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 25,775 of which males and females were 13,388 and 12,387. This child population figure of Madhubani district is 15.75 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Madhubani district as per census 2011 is 71.06 % of which males and females are 78.81 % and 62.39 % literates respectively. In actual number 96,443 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 56,460 and 39,983 respectively.

Madhubani District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 96.40 % population of Madhubani districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Madhubani district population living in rural areas is 4,325,884 of which males and females are 2,244,287 and 2,081,597 respectively. In rural areas of Madhubani district, sex ratio is 928 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Madhubani district is considered, figure is 936 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 784,704 in rural areas of which males were 405,228 and females were 379,476. The child population comprises 18.06 % of total rural population of Madhubani district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Madhubani district is 58.14 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 69.80 and 45.55 percent respectively. In total, 2,058,895 people were literate of which males and females were 1,283,625 and 775,270 respectively.
