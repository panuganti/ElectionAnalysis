Jehanabad District : Census 2011 data

Jehanabad District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Jehanabad, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Jehanabad District of Bihar.

Jehanabad District Population 2011

In 2011, Jehanabad had population of 1,125,313 of which male and female were 585,582 and 539,731 respectively. In 2001 census, Jehanabad had a population of 924,839 of which males were 480,301 and remaining 444,538 were females. Jehanabad District population constituted 1.08 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Jehanabad District was at 1.11 percent of Maharashtra population.

Jehanabad District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 21.68 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Jehanabad District recorded increase of 28.29 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Jehanabad District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Jehanabad district for 2011 is 1,209 people per sq. km. In 2001, Jehanabad district density was at 992 people per sq. km. Jehanabad district administers 931 square kilometers of areas.

Jehanabad Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Jehanabad in 2011 were 66.80 compared to 55.22 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 77.66 and 55.01 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 69.52 and 39.82 in Jehanabad District. Total literate in Jehanabad District were 618,881 of which male and female were 374,412 and 244,469 respectively. In 2001, Jehanabad District had 411,510 in its district.

Jehanabad Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Jehanabad, it stood at 922 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 926. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 922 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 915 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Jehanabad Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Jehanabad. There were total 198,785 children under age of 0-6 against 179,692 of 2001 census. Of total 198,785 male and female were 103,452 and 95,333 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 922 compared to 915 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 17.66 percent of Jehanabad District compared to 19.43 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.77 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Jehanabad District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Jehanabad population for 2011 census, 12.01 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 135,196 people lives in urban areas of which males are 71,428 and females are 63,768. Sex Ratio in urban region of Jehanabad district is 893 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Jehanabad district was 901 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 20,634 of which males and females were 10,857 and 9,777. This child population figure of Jehanabad district is 15.20 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Jehanabad district as per census 2011 is 76.33 % of which males and females are 83.00 % and 68.84 % literates respectively. In actual number 87,440 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 50,272 and 37,168 respectively.

Jehanabad District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 87.99 % population of Jehanabad districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Jehanabad district population living in rural areas is 990,117 of which males and females are 514,154 and 475,963 respectively. In rural areas of Jehanabad district, sex ratio is 926 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Jehanabad district is considered, figure is 924 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 178,151 in rural areas of which males were 92,595 and females were 85,556. The child population comprises 18.01 % of total rural population of Jehanabad district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Jehanabad district is 65.45 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 76.89 and 53.10 percent respectively. In total, 531,441 people were literate of which males and females were 324,140 and 207,301 respectively.
