Araria District : Census 2011 data

Araria District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Araria, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Araria District of Bihar.

Araria District Population 2011

In 2011, Araria had population of 2,811,569 of which male and female were 1,463,333 and 1,348,236 respectively. In 2001 census, Araria had a population of 2,158,608 of which males were 1,128,105 and remaining 1,030,503 were females. Araria District population constituted 2.70 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Araria District was at 2.60 percent of Maharashtra population.

Araria District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 30.25 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Araria District recorded increase of 31.84 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Araria District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Araria district for 2011 is 993 people per sq. km. In 2001, Araria district density was at 763 people per sq. km. Araria district administers 2,830 square kilometers of areas.

Araria Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Araria in 2011 were 53.53 compared to 35.01 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 62.30 and 43.93 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 46.38 and 22.37 in Araria District. Total literate in Araria District were 1,195,768 of which male and female were 727,643 and 468,125 respectively. In 2001, Araria District had 589,679 in its district.

Araria Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Araria, it stood at 921 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 913. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 957 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 963 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Araria Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Araria. There were total 577,886 children under age of 0-6 against 474,181 of 2001 census. Of total 577,886 male and female were 295,315 and 282,571 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 957 compared to 963 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 20.55 percent of Araria District compared to 21.97 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.42 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Araria District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Araria population for 2011 census, 6.00 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 168,777 people lives in urban areas of which males are 89,079 and females are 79,698. Sex Ratio in urban region of Araria district is 895 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Araria district was 953 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 28,185 of which males and females were 14,432 and 13,753. This child population figure of Araria district is 16.20 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Araria district as per census 2011 is 72.10 % of which males and females are 77.80 % and 65.65 % literates respectively. In actual number 101,365 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 58,075 and 43,290 respectively.

Araria District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 94.00 % population of Araria districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Araria district population living in rural areas is 2,642,792 of which males and females are 1,374,254 and 1,268,538 respectively. In rural areas of Araria district, sex ratio is 923 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Araria district is considered, figure is 957 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 549,701 in rural areas of which males were 280,883 and females were 268,818. The child population comprises 20.44 % of total rural population of Araria district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Araria district is 52.29 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 61.24 and 42.50 percent respectively. In total, 1,094,403 people were literate of which males and females were 669,568 and 424,835 respectively.
