Buxar District : Census 2011 data

Buxar District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Buxar, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Buxar District of Bihar.

Buxar District Population 2011

In 2011, Buxar had population of 1,706,352 of which male and female were 887,977 and 818,375 respectively. In 2001 census, Buxar had a population of 1,402,396 of which males were 738,354 and remaining 664,042 were females. Buxar District population constituted 1.64 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Buxar District was at 1.69 percent of Maharashtra population.

Buxar District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 21.67 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Buxar District recorded increase of 29.03 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Buxar District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Buxar district for 2011 is 1,002 people per sq. km. In 2001, Buxar district density was at 823 people per sq. km. Buxar district administers 1,703 square kilometers of areas.

Buxar Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Buxar in 2011 were 70.14 compared to 56.80 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 80.72 and 58.63 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 71.92 and 39.88 in Buxar District. Total literate in Buxar District were 989,807 of which male and female were 593,544 and 396,263 respectively. In 2001, Buxar District had 642,167 in its district.

Buxar Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Buxar, it stood at 922 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 899. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 934 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 925 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Buxar Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Buxar. There were total 295,127 children under age of 0-6 against 271,849 of 2001 census. Of total 295,127 male and female were 152,626 and 142,501 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 934 compared to 925 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 17.30 percent of Buxar District compared to 19.38 percent of 2001. There was net change of -2.08 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Buxar District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Buxar population for 2011 census, 9.64 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 164,499 people lives in urban areas of which males are 86,891 and females are 77,608. Sex Ratio in urban region of Buxar district is 893 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Buxar district was 906 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 23,882 of which males and females were 12,530 and 11,352. This child population figure of Buxar district is 14.42 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Buxar district as per census 2011 is 79.38 % of which males and females are 85.34 % and 72.70 % literates respectively. In actual number 111,627 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 63,461 and 48,166 respectively.

Buxar District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 90.36 % population of Buxar districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Buxar district population living in rural areas is 1,541,853 of which males and females are 801,086 and 740,767 respectively. In rural areas of Buxar district, sex ratio is 925 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Buxar district is considered, figure is 936 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 271,245 in rural areas of which males were 140,096 and females were 131,149. The child population comprises 17.49 % of total rural population of Buxar district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Buxar district is 69.11 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 80.20 and 57.10 percent respectively. In total, 878,180 people were literate of which males and females were 530,083 and 348,097 respectively.
