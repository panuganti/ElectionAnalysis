Jalpura Population - Patna, Bihar

Overview

Jalpura is small village located in Paliganj Block of Patna district, Bihar with total 216 families residing. The Jalpura village has population of 1570 of which 828 are males while 742 are females as per Population Census 2011.

In Jalpura village population of children with age 0-6 is 245 which makes up 15.61 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Jalpura village is 896 which is lower than Bihar state average of 918. Child Sex Ratio for the Jalpura as per census is 750, lower than Bihar average of 935.

Jalpura village has higher literacy rate compared to Bihar. In 2011, literacy rate of Jalpura village was 81.89 % compared to 61.80 % of Bihar. In Jalpura Male literacy stands at 90.41 % while female literacy rate was 72.68 %.

As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Jalpura village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.

Caste Factor

Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 5.80 % of total population in Jalpura village. The village Jalpura currently doesn’t have any Schedule Tribe (ST) population.

Work Profile

In Jalpura village out of total population, 406 were engaged in work activities. 40.89 % of workers describe their work as Main Work (Employment or Earning more than 6 Months) while 59.11 % were involved in Marginal activity providing livelihood for less than 6 months. Of 406 workers engaged in Main Work, 89 were cultivators (owner or co-owner) while 20 were Agricultural labourer.
