Pashchim Champaran (West Champaran) District : Census 2011 data

Pashchim Champaran District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Pashchim Champaran (West Champaran), a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Pashchim Champaran District of Bihar.

Pashchim Champaran District Population 2011

In 2011, Pashchim Champaran had population of 3,935,042 of which male and female were 2,061,110 and 1,873,932 respectively. In 2001 census, Pashchim Champaran had a population of 3,043,466 of which males were 1,600,839 and remaining 1,442,627 were females. Pashchim Champaran District population constituted 3.78 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Pashchim Champaran District was at 3.67 percent of Maharashtra population.

Pashchim Champaran District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 29.29 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Pashchim Champaran District recorded increase of 30.40 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Pashchim Champaran District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Pashchim Champaran district for 2011 is 753 people per sq. km. In 2001, Pashchim Champaran district density was at 582 people per sq. km. Pashchim Champaran district administers 5,228 square kilometers of areas.

Pashchim Champaran Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Pashchim Champaran in 2011 were 55.70 compared to 38.93 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 65.59 and 44.69 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 51.08 and 25.23 in Pashchim Champaran District. Total literate in Pashchim Champaran District were 1,759,481 of which male and female were 1,091,226 and 668,255 respectively. In 2001, Pashchim Champaran District had 932,347 in its district.

Pashchim Champaran Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Pashchim Champaran, it stood at 909 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 901. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 953 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 953 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Pashchim Champaran Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Pashchim Champaran. There were total 776,031 children under age of 0-6 against 648,358 of 2001 census. Of total 776,031 male and female were 397,418 and 378,613 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 953 compared to 953 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 19.72 percent of Pashchim Champaran District compared to 21.30 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.58 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Pashchim Champaran District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Pashchim Champaran population for 2011 census, 9.99 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 393,165 people lives in urban areas of which males are 207,301 and females are 185,864. Sex Ratio in urban region of Pashchim Champaran district is 897 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Pashchim Champaran district was 924 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 64,014 of which males and females were 33,263 and 30,751. This child population figure of Pashchim Champaran district is 16.05 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Pashchim Champaran district as per census 2011 is 71.12 % of which males and females are 77.47 % and 63.98 % literates respectively. In actual number 234,077 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 134,833 and 99,244 respectively.

Pashchim Champaran District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 90.01 % population of Pashchim Champaran districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Pashchim Champaran district population living in rural areas is 3,541,877 of which males and females are 1,853,809 and 1,688,068 respectively. In rural areas of Pashchim Champaran district, sex ratio is 911 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Pashchim Champaran district is considered, figure is 955 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 712,017 in rural areas of which males were 364,155 and females were 347,862. The child population comprises 19.64 % of total rural population of Pashchim Champaran district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Pashchim Champaran district is 53.90 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 64.20 and 42.46 percent respectively. In total, 1,525,404 people were literate of which males and females were 956,393 and 569,011 respectively.
