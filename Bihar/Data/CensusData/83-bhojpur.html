Bhojpur District : Census 2011 data

Bhojpur District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Bhojpur, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Bhojpur District of Bihar.

Bhojpur District Population 2011

In 2011, Bhojpur had population of 2,728,407 of which male and female were 1,430,380 and 1,298,027 respectively. In 2001 census, Bhojpur had a population of 2,243,144 of which males were 1,179,611 and remaining 1,063,533 were females. Bhojpur District population constituted 2.62 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Bhojpur District was at 2.70 percent of Maharashtra population.

Bhojpur District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 21.63 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Bhojpur District recorded increase of 24.58 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Bhojpur District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Bhojpur district for 2011 is 1,139 people per sq. km. In 2001, Bhojpur district density was at 937 people per sq. km. Bhojpur district administers 2,395 square kilometers of areas.

Bhojpur Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Bhojpur in 2011 were 70.47 compared to 58.96 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 81.74 and 58.03 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 74.29 and 41.80 in Bhojpur District. Total literate in Bhojpur District were 1,599,151 of which male and female were 973,486 and 625,665 respectively. In 2001, Bhojpur District had 1,073,010 in its district.

Bhojpur Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Bhojpur, it stood at 907 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 902. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 918 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 940 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Bhojpur Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Bhojpur. There were total 459,187 children under age of 0-6 against 423,242 of 2001 census. Of total 459,187 male and female were 239,364 and 219,823 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 918 compared to 940 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 16.83 percent of Bhojpur District compared to 18.87 percent of 2001. There was net change of -2.04 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Bhojpur District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Bhojpur population for 2011 census, 14.29 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 389,861 people lives in urban areas of which males are 206,106 and females are 183,755. Sex Ratio in urban region of Bhojpur district is 892 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Bhojpur district was 904 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 56,918 of which males and females were 29,900 and 27,018. This child population figure of Bhojpur district is 14.51 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Bhojpur district as per census 2011 is 78.12 % of which males and females are 84.43 % and 71.02 % literates respectively. In actual number 260,088 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 148,767 and 111,321 respectively.

Bhojpur District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 85.71 % population of Bhojpur districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Bhojpur district population living in rural areas is 2,338,546 of which males and females are 1,224,274 and 1,114,272 respectively. In rural areas of Bhojpur district, sex ratio is 910 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Bhojpur district is considered, figure is 920 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 402,269 in rural areas of which males were 209,464 and females were 192,805. The child population comprises 17.11 % of total rural population of Bhojpur district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Bhojpur district is 69.16 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 81.27 and 55.82 percent respectively. In total, 1,339,063 people were literate of which males and females were 824,719 and 514,344 respectively.
