Madhepura District : Census 2011 data

Madhepura District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Madhepura, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Madhepura District of Bihar.

Madhepura District Population 2011

In 2011, Madhepura had population of 2,001,762 of which male and female were 1,047,559 and 954,203 respectively. In 2001 census, Madhepura had a population of 1,526,646 of which males were 797,180 and remaining 729,466 were females. Madhepura District population constituted 1.92 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Madhepura District was at 1.84 percent of Maharashtra population.

Madhepura District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 31.12 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Madhepura District recorded increase of 29.45 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Madhepura District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Madhepura district for 2011 is 1,120 people per sq. km. In 2001, Madhepura district density was at 854 people per sq. km. Madhepura district administers 1,788 square kilometers of areas.

Madhepura Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Madhepura in 2011 were 52.25 compared to 36.07 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 61.77 and 41.74 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 48.80 and 22.11 in Madhepura District. Total literate in Madhepura District were 834,577 of which male and female were 517,666 and 316,911 respectively. In 2001, Madhepura District had 431,480 in its district.

Madhepura Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Madhepura, it stood at 911 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 915. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 930 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 927 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Madhepura Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Madhepura. There were total 404,576 children under age of 0-6 against 330,409 of 2001 census. Of total 404,576 male and female were 209,571 and 195,005 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 930 compared to 927 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 20.21 percent of Madhepura District compared to 21.64 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.43 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Madhepura District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Madhepura population for 2011 census, 4.42 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 88,461 people lives in urban areas of which males are 46,799 and females are 41,662. Sex Ratio in urban region of Madhepura district is 890 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Madhepura district was 942 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 14,314 of which males and females were 7,372 and 6,942. This child population figure of Madhepura district is 15.75 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Madhepura district as per census 2011 is 73.50 % of which males and females are 79.79 % and 66.35 % literates respectively. In actual number 54,496 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 31,459 and 23,037 respectively.

Madhepura District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 95.58 % population of Madhepura districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Madhepura district population living in rural areas is 1,913,301 of which males and females are 1,000,760 and 912,541 respectively. In rural areas of Madhepura district, sex ratio is 912 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Madhepura district is considered, figure is 930 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 390,262 in rural areas of which males were 202,199 and females were 188,063. The child population comprises 20.20 % of total rural population of Madhepura district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Madhepura district is 51.22 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 60.89 and 40.56 percent respectively. In total, 780,081 people were literate of which males and females were 486,207 and 293,874 respectively.
