Muraha Population - Samastipur, Bihar

Overview

Muraha is small village located in Hasanpur Block of Samastipur district, Bihar with total 575 families residing. The Muraha village has population of 2919 of which 1530 are males while 1389 are females as per Population Census 2011.

In Muraha village population of children with age 0-6 is 685 which makes up 23.47 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Muraha village is 908 which is lower than Bihar state average of 918. Child Sex Ratio for the Muraha as per census is 851, lower than Bihar average of 935.

Muraha village has lower literacy rate compared to Bihar. In 2011, literacy rate of Muraha village was 50.04 % compared to 61.80 % of Bihar. In Muraha Male literacy stands at 59.66 % while female literacy rate was 39.66 %.

As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Muraha village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.

Caste Factor

In Muraha village, most of the villagers are from Schedule Caste (SC). Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 47.52 % of total population in Muraha village. The village Muraha currently doesn’t have any Schedule Tribe (ST) population.

Work Profile

In Muraha village out of total population, 1052 were engaged in work activities. 58.27 % of workers describe their work as Main Work (Employment or Earning more than 6 Months) while 41.73 % were involved in Marginal activity providing livelihood for less than 6 months. Of 1052 workers engaged in Main Work, 149 were cultivators (owner or co-owner) while 275 were Agricultural labourer.
