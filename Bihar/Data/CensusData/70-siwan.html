Siwan District : Census 2011 data

Siwan District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Siwan, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Siwan District of Bihar.

Siwan District Population 2011

In 2011, Siwan had population of 3,330,464 of which male and female were 1,675,090 and 1,655,374 respectively. In 2001 census, Siwan had a population of 2,714,349 of which males were 1,336,283 and remaining 1,378,066 were females. Siwan District population constituted 3.20 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Siwan District was at 3.27 percent of Maharashtra population.

Siwan District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 22.70 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Siwan District recorded increase of 24.78 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Siwan District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Siwan district for 2011 is 1,501 people per sq. km. In 2001, Siwan district density was at 1,223 people per sq. km. Siwan district administers 2,219 square kilometers of areas.

Siwan Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Siwan in 2011 were 69.45 compared to 51.65 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 80.23 and 58.66 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 67.26 and 36.88 in Siwan District. Total literate in Siwan District were 1,930,175 of which male and female were 1,115,906 and 814,269 respectively. In 2001, Siwan District had 1,118,027 in its district.

Siwan Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Siwan, it stood at 988 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 1031. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 940 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 934 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Siwan Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Siwan. There were total 551,418 children under age of 0-6 against 549,611 of 2001 census. Of total 551,418 male and female were 284,188 and 267,230 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 940 compared to 934 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 16.56 percent of Siwan District compared to 20.25 percent of 2001. There was net change of -3.69 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Siwan District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Siwan population for 2011 census, 5.49 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 182,913 people lives in urban areas of which males are 95,405 and females are 87,508. Sex Ratio in urban region of Siwan district is 917 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Siwan district was 904 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 25,458 of which males and females were 13,372 and 12,086. This child population figure of Siwan district is 14.02 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Siwan district as per census 2011 is 77.93 % of which males and females are 84.10 % and 71.22 % literates respectively. In actual number 122,709 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 68,990 and 53,719 respectively.

Siwan District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 94.51 % population of Siwan districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Siwan district population living in rural areas is 3,147,551 of which males and females are 1,579,685 and 1,567,866 respectively. In rural areas of Siwan district, sex ratio is 993 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Siwan district is considered, figure is 942 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 525,960 in rural areas of which males were 270,816 and females were 255,144. The child population comprises 17.14 % of total rural population of Siwan district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Siwan district is 68.95 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 79.99 and 57.94 percent respectively. In total, 1,807,466 people were literate of which males and females were 1,046,916 and 760,550 respectively.
