Bhagalpur District : Census 2011 data

Bhagalpur District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Bhagalpur, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Bhagalpur District of Bihar.

Bhagalpur District Population 2011

In 2011, Bhagalpur had population of 3,037,766 of which male and female were 1,615,663 and 1,422,103 respectively. In 2001 census, Bhagalpur had a population of 2,423,172 of which males were 1,291,658 and remaining 1,131,514 were females. Bhagalpur District population constituted 2.92 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Bhagalpur District was at 2.92 percent of Maharashtra population.

Bhagalpur District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 25.36 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Bhagalpur District recorded increase of 27.24 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Bhagalpur District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Bhagalpur district for 2011 is 1,182 people per sq. km. In 2001, Bhagalpur district density was at 943 people per sq. km. Bhagalpur district administers 2,569 square kilometers of areas.

Bhagalpur Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Bhagalpur in 2011 were 63.14 compared to 49.50 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 70.30 and 54.89 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 59.22 and 38.13 in Bhagalpur District. Total literate in Bhagalpur District were 1,573,066 of which male and female were 937,669 and 635,397 respectively. In 2001, Bhagalpur District had 965,683 in its district.

Bhagalpur Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Bhagalpur, it stood at 880 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 876. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 938 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 966 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Bhagalpur Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Bhagalpur. There were total 546,382 children under age of 0-6 against 472,126 of 2001 census. Of total 546,382 male and female were 281,937 and 264,445 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 938 compared to 966 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 17.99 percent of Bhagalpur District compared to 19.48 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.49 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Bhagalpur District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Bhagalpur population for 2011 census, 19.83 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 602,532 people lives in urban areas of which males are 320,277 and females are 282,255. Sex Ratio in urban region of Bhagalpur district is 881 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Bhagalpur district was 915 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 89,372 of which males and females were 46,671 and 42,701. This child population figure of Bhagalpur district is 14.57 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Bhagalpur district as per census 2011 is 75.87 % of which males and females are 80.73 % and 70.33 % literates respectively. In actual number 389,360 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 220,879 and 168,481 respectively.

Bhagalpur District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 80.17 % population of Bhagalpur districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Bhagalpur district population living in rural areas is 2,435,234 of which males and females are 1,295,386 and 1,139,848 respectively. In rural areas of Bhagalpur district, sex ratio is 880 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Bhagalpur district is considered, figure is 943 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 457,010 in rural areas of which males were 235,266 and females were 221,744. The child population comprises 18.16 % of total rural population of Bhagalpur district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Bhagalpur district is 59.84 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 67.61 and 50.86 percent respectively. In total, 1,183,706 people were literate of which males and females were 716,790 and 466,916 respectively.
