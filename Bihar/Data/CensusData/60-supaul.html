Supaul District : Census 2011 data

Supaul District Overview

An official Census 2011 detail of Supaul, a district of Bihar has been released by Directorate of Census Operations in Bihar. Enumeration of key persons was also done by census officials in Supaul District of Bihar.

Supaul District Population 2011

In 2011, Supaul had population of 2,229,076 of which male and female were 1,155,283 and 1,073,793 respectively. In 2001 census, Supaul had a population of 1,732,578 of which males were 902,207 and remaining 830,371 were females. Supaul District population constituted 2.14 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Supaul District was at 2.09 percent of Maharashtra population.

Supaul District Population Growth Rate

There was change of 28.66 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Supaul District recorded increase of 29.95 percent to its population compared to 1991.

Supaul District Density 2011

The initial provisional data released by census India 2011, shows that density of Supaul district for 2011 is 919 people per sq. km. In 2001, Supaul district density was at 714 people per sq. km. Supaul district administers 2,425 square kilometers of areas.

Supaul Literacy Rate 2011

Average literacy rate of Supaul in 2011 were 57.67 compared to 37.28 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 69.62 and 44.77 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 52.42 and 20.81 in Supaul District. Total literate in Supaul District were 1,033,283 of which male and female were 647,672 and 385,611 respectively. In 2001, Supaul District had 509,025 in its district.

Supaul Sex Ratio 2011

With regards to Sex Ratio in Supaul, it stood at 929 per 1000 male compared to 2001 census figure of 920. The average national sex ratio in India is 940 as per latest reports of Census 2011 Directorate. In 2011 census, child sex ratio is 944 girls per 1000 boys compared to figure of 925 girls per 1000 boys of 2001 census data.

Supaul Child Population 2011

In census enumeration, data regarding child under 0-6 age were also collected for all districts including Supaul. There were total 437,357 children under age of 0-6 against 367,302 of 2001 census. Of total 437,357 male and female were 224,953 and 212,404 respectively. Child Sex Ratio as per census 2011 was 944 compared to 925 of census 2001. In 2011, Children under 0-6 formed 19.62 percent of Supaul District compared to 21.20 percent of 2001. There was net change of -1.58 percent in this compared to previous census of India.

Supaul District Urban Population 2011

Out of the total Supaul population for 2011 census, 4.74 percent lives in urban regions of district. In total 105,558 people lives in urban areas of which males are 55,788 and females are 49,770. Sex Ratio in urban region of Supaul district is 892 as per 2011 census data. Similarly child sex ratio in Supaul district was 932 in 2011 census. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 17,654 of which males and females were 9,140 and 8,514. This child population figure of Supaul district is 16.38 % of total urban population. Average literacy rate in Supaul district as per census 2011 is 72.74 % of which males and females are 80.78 % and 63.64 % literates respectively. In actual number 63,939 people are literate in urban region of which males and females are 37,684 and 26,255 respectively.

Supaul District Rural Population 2011

As per 2011 census, 95.26 % population of Supaul districts lives in rural areas of villages. The total Supaul district population living in rural areas is 2,123,518 of which males and females are 1,099,495 and 1,024,023 respectively. In rural areas of Supaul district, sex ratio is 931 females per 1000 males. If child sex ratio data of Supaul district is considered, figure is 945 girls per 1000 boys. Child population in the age 0-6 is 419,703 in rural areas of which males were 215,813 and females were 203,890. The child population comprises 19.63 % of total rural population of Supaul district. Literacy rate in rural areas of Supaul district is 56.89 % as per census data 2011. Gender wise, male and female literacy stood at 69.03 and 43.82 percent respectively. In total, 969,344 people were literate of which males and females were 609,988 and 359,356 respectively.
