Jogabani Population Census 2011

Overview

Jogabani is a Nagar Panchayat city in district of Araria, Bihar. The Jogabani city is divided into 19 wards for which elections are held every 5 years. The Jogabani Nagar Panchayat has population of 39,281 of which 20,419 are males while 18,862 are females as per report released by Census India 2011.

Population of Children with age of 0-6 is 7113 which is 18.11 % of total population of Jogabani (NP). In Jogabani Nagar Panchayat, Female Sex Ratio is of 924 against state average of 918. Moreover Child Sex Ratio in Jogabani is around 975 compared to Bihar state average of 935. Literacy rate of Jogabani city is 62.74 % higher than state average of 61.80 %. In Jogabani, Male literacy is around 71.73 % while female literacy rate is 52.90 %.

Jogabani Nagar Panchayat has total administration over 7,901 houses to which it supplies basic amenities like water and sewerage. It is also authorize to build roads within Nagar Panchayat limits and impose taxes on properties coming under its jurisdiction.

Caste Factor

Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 12.44 % while Schedule Tribe (ST) were 0.24 % of total population in Jogabani (NP).

Work Profile

Out of total population, 12,731 were engaged in work or business activity. Of this 9,874 were males while 2,857 were females. In census survey, worker is defined as person who does business, job, service, and cultivator and labour activity. Of total 12731 working population, 85.95 % were engaged in Main Work while 14.05 % of total workers were engaged in Marginal Work.
